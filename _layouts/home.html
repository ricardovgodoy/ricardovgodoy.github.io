I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of São Paulo, working on manipulation and loco-manipulation for inspection and mobile robots. A lot of my work keeps a human in the loop: teleoperation and shared control, driven by wearable sensing (EMG/LMG) and external perception like motion capture and vision. The goal is usually to get these systems running in real time, outside the lab.
