I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of São Paulo working on manipulation and loco-manipulation for inspection and field robotics. Much of my work keeps a human in the loop — teleoperation and shared control driven by wearable sensing (EMG/LMG) and external perception (motion capture, vision) — with the aim of getting systems to run in real time, outside the lab.
