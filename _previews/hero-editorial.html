Research

I work end-to-end — perception, intent, control, and evaluation — so that capable AI actually reaches deployment on real robots. Current threads:

  • Dexterous manipulation in clutter
  • Teleoperation and shared control
  • Wearable sensing & biosignal-driven intent (EMG)
  • Vision–language models for locomanipulation
  • Assistive human–robot interaction
  • Locomanipulation on mobile platforms
  • Robot self-learning in the real world
